POLITICAL - Do I have political concerns?

Political considerations will always impact a project procurement decision. Such considerations include whether the project spans multiple political jurisdictions, whether the project includes or affects multiple politically active stakeholders, the degree of constituent pressure against new/increased taxes, rate increases, and new user fees and charges; the likelihood of constituent dissatisfaction from project completion delays, the likelihood of constituent fallout from a failure of the project to work as designed/constructed or from a failure of technology to perform as promised, and your ability to absorb material project cost overruns.
